The Team

With years of experience in digital marketing agencies and supporting clients directly, Mark Reeves created Wintergreen Digital to serve as a single point of contact for growing businesses with evolving marketing & technology needs.

From website recipe management for Campbell’s Kitchen to marketing support for Russell Morin Catering & Events and CRM integrations for Farm Collective Wine, Mark knows how to help brands better connect with their target market. From concept to launch, Mark applies a big picture approach to brand marketing, supporting happy & engaged customers, staff, and sales teams.
